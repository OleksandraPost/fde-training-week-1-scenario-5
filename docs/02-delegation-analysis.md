# Delegation Analysis

## Fully Agentic

- Insurance verification
- Prior authorization checks
- Task creation and tracking
- Notifications and reminders

Reason:
Deterministic, repeatable, low-risk operations

---

## Agent-led with Human Oversight

- Visit reason capture and flagging
- Medication change reporting
- Allergy confirmation

Reason:
Involves ambiguity or impacts clinical data

---

## Human-led

- Clinical triage decisions
- Final medication/allergy updates
- Policy overrides

Reason:
Requires accountability and domain expertise
